Questions

Frequently asked

Is Pathwise free to use?
Yes — creating a profile and receiving AI-matched recommendations is completely free. Premium features like application tracking and advisor consultations are available on paid plans.
How does the AI matching actually work?
We combine your academic profile, test scores, work experience, location preferences, and financial situation against a structured database of over 12,000 programs. A compatibility score is computed for each program based on how closely your profile aligns with typical admitted students.
Do I need GRE/GMAT scores to get recommendations?
No. If you don't have test scores yet, we filter for programs that are test-optional. You can always update your profile later once you have scores.
How is my data handled?
Your profile and documents are encrypted at rest. We never share your data with universities or third parties without explicit consent. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
How long does the questionnaire take?
Most students complete all 5 sections in 7–10 minutes. You can save progress and return at any time.
